Welcome to Make A Splash Foundation's Donation Page

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to preventing childhood drowning by raising awareness and providing scholarships for survival swim lessons to families in need.

With your support, we can make a real difference in the lives of children and families by providing scholarships for survival swim lessons to prevent drowning accidents. Every donation counts and helps us save lives.

Thank you for your generosity and for joining us in our mission to prevent childhood drowning.

Your Impact

Drowning is the #1 cause of accidental death for young children. Together, we can change that.

The Make A Splash Foundation provides survival swim scholarships to families who cannot afford lessons. Your support makes it possible for every child to learn life-saving water skills.
