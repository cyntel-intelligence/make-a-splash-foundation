Our Mission

Making Water Safety Accessible to Every Child

The Make A Splash Foundation prevents childhood drowning by providing scholarships for survival swim lessons to families who cannot afford them. We believe that financial constraints should never be a barrier to water safety.

Drowning is the leading cause of accidental death among children ages 1-4. Through community partnerships and generous donors, we're creating a safer future, one child at a time. Together, we can Make A Splash and save lives.

Our Impact

Funding life-saving swim education for families in need

Make A Splash Foundation partners with certified survival swim schools to provide scholarships for children whose families face financial barriers to water safety education. We bridge the gap between families in need and life-saving instruction.

1

Families Apply

Families facing financial hardship submit a simple scholarship application through our website.

2

We Fund Lessons

Approved applicants receive scholarships covering survival swim lessons at our partner schools.

3

Children Learn

Children receive professional survival swim instruction from certified instructors at partner facilities.
