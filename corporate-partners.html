Frequently Asked Questions

Are corporate donations tax-deductible?

Yes! Make A Splash Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (Tax ID: 92-3713877). All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. We provide detailed receipts for your accounting department.

How will my donation be used?

100% of your donation goes directly to funding survival swim lessons for families who cannot afford them. We provide detailed impact reports showing exactly how many children benefited from your support.

Can we customize our partnership?

Absolutely! We're flexible and happy to create a custom partnership package that meets your specific goals, whether that's employee engagement, brand visibility, or community impact.

Do you offer multi-year partnerships?

Yes! Multi-year commitments allow us to plan more effectively and often come with enhanced benefits and preferred rates. Contact us to discuss options.

Can our employees get involved?

Yes! We offer volunteer opportunities at events, donation matching programs, and ways for your team to engage directly with the families we serve. Employee engagement is a key benefit of corporate partnership.
